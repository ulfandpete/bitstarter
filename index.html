Most price comparison sites today are ugly, crammed with stuff you do not need and full of useless advertisment. At the same time they ask for money from the shops they list, thus they are biased by design. Wushel is different. It is a simple, independent price comparison engine that is fast and easy to use. In addition it includes unique features like the possibility to search for multiple products at the same time and calculate the cheapest combination of product prices and shipping fees.

Wushel is a fully featured next generation price comparison portal. Key features include:

  • compare multiple products at the same time and caclulate the cheapest combination of which items to buy in which store
  • price comparison includes shipping fees
  • includes special deals from mydealz.com
  • no need to register at every store. Just enter your personal information once
  • track & trace your orders - all in one place
